About us

Simbi Roses Farm

Simbi Rose farm is located in Thika, an hours drive from capital city Nairobi. This proximity to Nairobi, the climate and constant water provision via several man-made dams in the area make the region popular with the agricultural industry. Since its inception, Simbi Roses have established a modern and efficient crop management system and high quality control within our growing infrastructure.

Our Mission

the Passion and love for Flowers

In 1995, One woman's Love of flowers began the story of a small 2 hectare farm, situated in the middle of a large coffee plantation. Over the last 25 years, both the passion for roses and the farm itself have expanded to a almost 30 hectares. Simbi is now able to produce up to two hundred thousand stems per day for the international market. And we Keep growing.

We are commited to our clients as we respect the enviroment and community.Our Highly skilled staff has enabled us to produce 25 rose varieties and we keep growing
